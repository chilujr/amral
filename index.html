ABOUT US

At Amral Inc., we empower Canadians to transition seamlessly from renting to owning their homes. We understand that each journey to homeownership is unique, and we're dedicated to making that goal attainable for everyone, regardless of where they start. Through our rent-to-own program, we bridge the gap by providing a clear path forward, offering flexible, transparent solutions tailored to individual needs. With Amral Inc., the dream of owning a home is more than a possibility—it’s within reach. Let us help you take that step toward a brighter, more secure future.


Our Mission

Our mission is to empower renters on their path to homeownership through a straightforward, supportive journey. Whether you’re building credit or saving for a down payment, we’re here to guide you at each step with clear tools, expert advice, and a personalized approach. Our team is committed to making homeownership accessible and achievable, helping you secure a home you’ll love with confidence and peace of mind.

HOW DOES RENT-TO-OWN WORK WITH AMRAL

  Step 1: Lease Agreement

Sign a rental agreement to live in the property

  Step 2: Option to Purchase

Get the exclusive right to buy the property within a set timeframe and price

  Step 3: Monthly Payment

A portion of monthly payments go towards the future downpayment



BENEFITS OF RENT-TO-OWN

  Less Upfront Costs

Move in with lower upfront costs compared to a traditional down payment.

  Build Home Equity

A portion of the rent goes towards the down payment, allowing you to build equity.

  Improve Finances Over Time

Over the lease period, improve credit & finances to secure a favorable mortgage.

  Avoid Speculation Tax

Avoid non-resident speculation taxes on purchases.

WE OFFER THE FOLLOWING SERVICES

Home Selection Assistance


  • Help clients find suitable homes within their budget and in desired locations.
  • Offer resources or partnerships with real estate agents to expand home options.

    • Credit Assessment and Improvement Programs

  • Evaluate clients’ credit to determine eligibility for the rent-to-own program.
  • Provide credit counseling and assistance to help clients improve their credit scores, making it easier to qualify for a mortgage later on.

    • Financial Planning and Down Payment Assistance

  • Develop a customized financial plan that includes rent credits or other savings programs to help clients save for a future down payment.
  • Offer budgeting tools or financial advice to ensure clients stay on track to afford the eventual purchase.

    • Flexible Leasing Agreements with Purchase Options


  • Provide lease agreements that include an option to purchase the property at a later date, often at a pre-agreed price.
  • Offer flexibility in lease terms that allow clients to secure the property while they work toward ownership.

    • Educational Resources and Guidance


  • Offer educational programs or materials on homeownership, the mortgage process, and financial literacy.
  • Regularly communicate with clients to answer questions and provide ongoing support, ensuring they understand the process and their responsibilities.

    • Legal and Transactional Support


  • Prepare all legal documents related to the rent-to-own agreement, including lease and purchase contracts.
  • Assist clients in navigating the legal and transactional steps needed to exercise their purchase option.
