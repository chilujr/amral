HOW DOES RENT-TO-OWN WORK

Step 1: Lease Agreement

Sign a rental agreement to live in the property.

Step 2: Save for Down Payment

Start saving for your future down payment with monthly option consideration contributions.

Step 3: Purchase the Property

At the end of the rental period, exercise your right to purchase the property with your saved funds.



BENEFITS OF RENT-TO-OWN

Less Upfront Costs

Move in with lower upfront costs compared to a traditional down payment.

Improve Finances Over Time

Over the lease period, improve credit & finances to secure a favorable mortgage.

Avoid Speculation Tax

Avoid non-resident speculation taxes on purchases.

ABOUT US

At Amral Inc., we help Canadians transition from renting to owning their homes. Every path to homeownership is unique, and we’re committed to making that goal attainable for everyone. Through our rent-to-own program, we provide a clear path forward with flexible, transparent solutions tailored to your needs. With us, your dream of owning a home isn’t just possible—it’s within reach.


Our mission is to empower renters on their journey to homeownership by providing clear guidance, personalized tools, and expert support. We’re dedicated to making homeownership accessible, helping you secure a home you’ll love with confidence and peace of mind.


The Team

Founded in Toronto, Ontario, in 2020, Amral is driven by dedication and a genuine commitment to helping people. With honesty and transparency at the core of our process, we aim to create win-win opportunities for tenant-buyers seeking to step into their dream homes today.


WE OFFER THE FOLLOWING SERVICES

1. Customized Rent-to-Own Plans

We create tailored plans that fit your financial situation, allowing you to rent now and purchase later. We guarantee regular communication and support so you always know what's next.

2. Credit Support & Financial Guidance

We provide credit counseling and assistance to help improve your credit score, making it easier to qualify for a mortgage and secure favorable terms later on.

3. A Variety of Homes to Choose From

Explore a wide selection of homes within your budget and preferences, in your desired locations. Start your journey today—contact us to learn more!
